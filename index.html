WHO ARE WE

DIEPIRIYE SHIPPING LTD. is an indegineous shipping company offering all inclusive range of ship and cargo related services in Africa and beyound. The company is also involved in vessel management and brokerage services especially in tanker handling. We are situated in Port Harcourt at No. 10 Jane Doe street,Port Harcourt Rivers State. Our professional experience in chartering of ocean going Petroleum Tankers and and supertankers cannot be equated. We have facilities for transporting Petroleum Product like CRUDE, LNG, LPK, LPG, DPK, PMS & AGO. We can provide logistics services for floating Production Storage and Offloading vessels, FPSO, tanker vessels, tug boat towage and mooring services.

Our ship to ship (S.T.S) transfer operation involving sea going ships positioned alongside each other while stationery orb underway has been adjudgeed one of the best. we transfer cargoes like crude oil, liquified natural and petroleum gas, bulk cargo and other petoleum products. We provide vessel managementand brokerage of major oil company approved vessels and large mother vessels. we also provide services to individual and commercial customers. Our expert Staff have the capacity to handle petoleum tanker or marine operation to load, store and transport petroleum produccts.
